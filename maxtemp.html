Countries on or near the equator don’t experience the exact same weather. Whilst the temperatures remain high, some countries experience wet and dry seasons. Countries on, or near, the equator have high temperatures all year round due to the tilt of the Earth’s axis.

As expected, the weather becomes signficantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 deg. latitude). Due to the tilt of the earth's axis at this time of year, the Southern Hemisphere temperatures are warmer than Northern Hemisphere temperatures.
